Garlic Butter Shrimp

Description

Garlic and butter. The two greatest pleasures in life. The two ingredients I could probably live off of for the rest of my life. And when you combine these two in a glorious shrimp skillet, well, it's nothing short of perfection.

  • garlic
  • butter
  • shrimp
  • olive oil
  • parsley
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. de-shell shrimp
  2. mix together seasoning
  3. toss shrimp in seasoning
  4. add olive oil to pre-heated pan
  5. cook shrimp 1 min each side
  6. add butter to pan with shrimp left on side until butter melted
  7. add shrimp to butter sauce
  8. add parsley to buttery shrimpy goodness
  9. serve
